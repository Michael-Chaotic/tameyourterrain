Agreement Terms

1. Fixed Price Agreement

This project is quoted as a fixed job price, not by the hour and not by the acre. The agreed price is based on Tame Your Terrain's site evaluation, expected conditions, access, and scope of work.

2. Deposit and Final Payment

A 20% non-refundable deposit is required before the project is placed on the schedule. The remaining balance is due within 7 days after project completion.

3. Scheduling and Delays

Start dates are estimates only. Weather, wet ground, equipment issues, hidden hazards, site access problems, and other unforeseen conditions may delay or pause work.

4. Site Marking and Customer Responsibility

The customer is responsible for identifying and clearly marking property lines, utilities, septic systems, wells, drain tile, buried wire, fencing, obstacles, and any other hidden hazards. Tame Your Terrain is not responsible for damage to unmarked items.

5. Surface Conditions and Results

Forestry mulching, land clearing, and trail work may leave uneven surfaces, tire marks, rutting, wood chips, and natural disturbance depending on weather and soil conditions. A finished lawn or smooth final grade is not included unless specifically stated in writing.

6. Additional Work

Any work outside the original agreed scope, including extra acreage, heavier-than-expected conditions, cleanup, or added finishing work, will require approval and additional cost.

7. Hidden Hazard and Equipment Damage Clause

If hidden metal, wire, concrete, buried debris, or other damaging materials are encountered, work may be paused. Damage, downtime, and repair costs caused by undisclosed hazards may be charged to the customer.

8. Cancellation

Once the project has been scheduled, the deposit is non-refundable.

9. Photo Release

Customer grants permission for Tame Your Terrain to take and use before-and-after project photos for marketing purposes, without sharing sensitive personal information.
