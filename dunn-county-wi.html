Forestry Mulching in Dunn County, WI

Tame Your Terrain provides forestry mulching, land clearing and UTV trail building throughout Dunn County, WI.

Habitat Improvement

Selective clearing can improve wildlife habitat by letting sunlight reach the forest floor, encouraging native plants that provide food and cover for deer and other wildlife.

Better habitat can also help improve bedding cover, edge habitat and travel corridors, while making the property easier to access and manage.
